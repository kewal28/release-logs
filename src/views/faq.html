FAQ

Why didn’t I receive a verification email?

Check spam. Your host must have SMTP configured via environment variables. See documentation.

Where do I configure S3 or SMTP?

In .env only—not in the database. Same for SaaS and self-host.

Is Redis required?

No. If REDIS_URL is unset, the app reads from MySQL only.

Where is the public changelog?

At /changelog.
